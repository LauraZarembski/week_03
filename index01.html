Shelley Conn specializes in all kinds of pain conditions, and has successfully treated numerous patients with back and neck pain, neuropathy, joint pain, sciatica, TMJ, facial pain, myositis, and bursitis. She combines acupuncture with Chinese bodywork, cupping, and electrostimulation to effectively and quickly relieve pain.

Shelley brings over 20 years in the health field to her practice of Traditional Chinese Medicine. She received her Masters of Oriental Medicine from the Minnesota College of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine at Northwestern Health Sciences University in Bloomington, Minnesota. In August of 2003, Shelley studied the Chinese healthcare system in several hospitals and clinics in China. She is part-time faculty at Northwestern Health Sciences University and supervises clinical interns in acupuncture at the Courage Center, a facility for physical rehabilitation.

Shelley also specializes in cosmetic acupuncture for the face, neck and abdomen, as well as stress and anxiety conditions. Shelley is passionate about the effectiveness of the healing properties of Traditional Chinese Medicine, and she brings that enthusiasm to her treatments. Shelley is certified in both Acupuncture and Chinese Herbology by the National Commission for the Certification of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine, and is licensed through the Minnesota State Board of Medical Practice. She is certified in the Mei Zen method of Cosmetic Acupuncture.
