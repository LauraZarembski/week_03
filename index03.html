Lynn Jaffee specializes in emotional conditons such as stress, anxiety and depression, and conditions that are aggravated by stress, lifestyle, or hormones, such as irritable bowel syndrome, headaches, pain conditions, PMS, and menopause symptoms. She also specializes in cosmetic acupuncture for the face and abdomen. Lynn is committed to helping people improve their health, Lynn Jaffeefeel better and have more energy. Her approach to treatment is holistic, compassionate and respectful.

Lynn has worked in the field of health promotion since 1985. She is the author of the book, Simple Steps: The Chinese Way to Better Health, has written numerous articles on health for a variety of publications, and co-authored the book The Bodywise Woman. Lynn received a Masters of Oriental Medicine from the Minnesota College of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine at Northwestern Health Sciences University in Bloomington, Minnesota. She also serves as Lecturer and Adjunct Clinical Faculty at the college. She holds a Bachelors degree in Health and Human Services. Lynn is certified in both Acupuncture and Chinese Herbology by the National Commission for the Certification of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine, and is licensed through the Minnesota State Board of Medical Practice. She is also certified in the Mei Zen method of cosmetic acupuncture.

Lynn is available as a public speaker on a variety of topics. She has presented to several groups of all sizes on topics related to acupuncture and Chinese medicine, such as aging, understanding acupuncture, and Chinese dietary theory.
